If you don't enjoy finding out how the game works, you may not be the gamer-type ;)


In a nutshell:

Worms eat bonuses which affect their length, health and direction. Besides affecting the points.

- Apples: Keep the game moving up levels

- Bananas: Lengthen worms

- Cherries: Shorten worms

- Diamonds: Change the direction of other worms

- Hearts: Add health


However, you can choose to play with fake bonuses.

In this case, most collectables are fake, they don't

influence the worm's length or points but change the direction of the worm that collected it.


If a real apple has not been picked up for a long period, it will vanish and another will apear. If more than one worm is competing and apples have had to re-spawn multiple times; each move will reduce all the worm's score.
